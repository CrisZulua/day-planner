How does it work?

What is DayPlanner?

DayPlanner is a web application that allows you to plan out your day. You can add, edit, and delete tasks as you like.

Your tasks will be display on a interactive timeline, where you can zoom in, zoom out, move tasks to a different point of time and change it's start time and end time.

Add new tasks

To add a new task, fill out the form and click the add button.

Whenever a new task is added, the timeline will automatically fit all tasks into the timeline.

Delete tasks

To delete a task, click on the task you want to delete to select it. Then click the delete button next to the task.

Edit tasks

To edit a task, double click on the task you want to edit and a form will be displayed with the task info to change.

If you accidentally double click on a task, just click outside of the form to close it.

Timeline features

Scroll Down: Zoom in on timeline.

Scroll Up: Zoom out on timeline.

Drag: Move timeline left/right.
